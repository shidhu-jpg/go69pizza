The Mastery of Heritage Recipes

Founded in 1994, Om Sai Sweets began with a single vision: to preserve the soul of traditional Indian mithai while elevating the presentation to match the sophistication of global palettes. What started as a small neighborhood kitchen has evolved into a hallmark of culinary excellence.

Our recipes are heirlooms, passed down through generations and refined with modern techniques. Every batch of Chenna, every simmer of Mawa, and every delicate fold of flaky pastry is a testament to our obsession with quality. We don't just make sweets; we craft experiences that evoke nostalgia and celebrate the present.
